The folder .../cities/scripts include codes to generate city objects.

city_generator.py is able to generate city objects with building nodes and
thermal/electrical loads within buildings (without streets, without energy
systems)

street_generatorpy is able to generate city objects with street networks only.
(no buildings, no energy systems)

complex_city_generator.py is able to generate city objects with buidling nodes,
loads within buildings and street networks (no energy systems)
